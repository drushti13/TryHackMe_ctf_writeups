# Letter

This CTF focused on gathering publicly available information using OSINT techniques. Each question required finding small clues and using different online resources to piece together the final answers.

I started by examining the files provided in the challenge. One of them was a handwritten note written in French. I first identified the language and then translated it into English to understand what it was about. The translated article mentioned a young man who had rescued people during an incident, which gave me a useful starting point.

To gather more information, I searched for historical newspaper archives and found the article on Wikipedia. I continued researching the incident and identified the person mentioned in the article. I then cross-referenced the surname with the information provided in the challenge until I found the correct answer.

For another task, I used the location mentioned in the article to identify the incident and easily obtained the corresponding postal code through online searches.

## Tools Used

* Google Search
* Google Translate
* Wikipedia
* Google Dorking

This challenge showed me how translating foreign-language documents, researching historical events, and cross-referencing information from multiple public sources can help solve OSINT challenges efficiently.
